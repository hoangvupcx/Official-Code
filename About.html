ABOUT US

First of its kind in Vietnam, Vietnam Vestonvux Shop aims to promote menswear tailoring that expresses a mix of elegance, quality and personal style.

We are advocators of the Sartorial way of dressing, originating from Europe at the beginning of the 20th century, that’s progressively spread to South Korea, followed by Japan, and that has today expanded throughout the world and is appreciated by many.

When promoting the Sartorial lifestyle, it seems indispensable to mention the Vestonvus industry. A vestonvux garment is a creation commissioned to a particular specification. It may be altered or tailored to the measurements, preferences, or usage of an individual purchaser

As such, each of our garment is unique and reserved for you only, and we work towards creating products that exuberates elegance but maintains a natural look. Our apparel is a harmonization between luxurious brands, the art of tailoring and the knowledge of fashion.

Each classic suit requires diligence, from its design, fabrication, to the attention that we put into caring for it after it’s been completed, as we propose premium laundry services and suit repair.

A Vestonvux suit is the result of multiple arrangements that allow our tailors to build on top of each fitting, achieving a more precise fit along the way.

You may customize it to your liking as vux tailoring offers a free ground for design and creativity. Not only do we offer a selection of the finest fabrics but we also have a wide range of buttons or lining options for you to choose from, among other details.
